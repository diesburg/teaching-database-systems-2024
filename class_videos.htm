I try to take video recordings of my voice and my laptop screen during each lecture, but please note that this is not an online class. Students do better with the material and earn higher grades by attending class in person. Class videos will not include any information written on the board, any in-class activities, or allow you to participate or ask questions during the lecture. That being said, these videos can be useful for you to:

  • Free up your attention during class and use the videos to take notes later.
  • Allow you to watch live coding in class and use the video to try the code later.
  • Serve as a source of notes if you are sick or have an exception where you cannot come to class.
